SECTION 1: SPEAKING TO A HUMAN AGENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q1: Can I speak to a real person instead of the chatbot?
A: Absolutely. If you prefer to speak with a human support agent, simply say
   "I want to speak to an agent" or "Connect me to a human" at any point during
   the chat. Our system will immediately flag your request and connect you with
   the right support team member based on your concern.

Q2: How do I request to escalate my issue to a manager?
A: You can request a manager at any time by typing something like "I want to
   speak to a manager" or "Please escalate this to a supervisor." Our system
   detects these requests automatically and routes your case to a Senior Support
   Agent or Support Manager depending on the nature of your issue.

Q3: Will the chatbot ever pass my issue to a human without me asking?
A: Yes. Our chatbot continuously monitors the conversation for signs of
   frustration, urgency, or unresolved problems. If it detects these signals —
   even if you haven't explicitly asked — it will automatically escalate your
   case to the appropriate team so a human can follow up with you promptly.

Q4: What hours can I reach a human support agent?
A: Human agents are available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. If you
   contact us outside these hours, your escalation will be logged and a team
   member will reach out to you on the next working day.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: HOW ESCALATION WORKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q5: What happens when my chat is escalated?
A: When your issue is escalated, our system:
   1. Captures your full conversation history for context.
   2. Assesses the seriousness of the issue (Level 1 to 4).
   3. Sends an automated alert email to the appropriate team member.
   4. That team member then reaches out to you via your registered contact.

Q6: What are the different escalation levels?
A: Smart Route Service uses a 4-tier escalation system:

   Level 1 — Tier 1 Support
   Triggered when the chatbot cannot find an answer to a general query and
   there is no sign of frustration or urgency. A basic support agent will
   follow up.

   Level 2 — Senior Support Agent
   Triggered when mild frustration is detected — e.g., the customer is
   dissatisfied but remains calm. A more experienced agent will handle it.

   Level 3 — Support Manager
   Triggered when there is clear anger, a repeated unresolved issue, or a
   financial/legal concern. A Support Manager will personally review and
   respond.

   Level 4 — Business Director
   Triggered in the most serious situations: abusive language, legal threats,
   safety concerns, or issues that could affect multiple customers. The
   Business Director is alerted immediately.

Q7: How quickly will someone get back to me after escalation?
A: Response times vary by level:
   - Level 1 & 2: Within 2–4 business hours during working hours.
   - Level 3: Within 1–2 business hours, priority handling.
   - Level 4: Immediate attention; you will be contacted as soon as possible.

Q8: Will I be informed when my issue has been escalated?
A: Yes. The chatbot will notify you in the chat that your issue has been
   escalated and that a team member will contact you. You may also receive
   a follow-up call or email on your registered contact details.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: FRUSTRATION, URGENCY & EMOTIONAL SIGNALS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q9: What if I'm very frustrated and the chatbot is not helping?
A: We understand how frustrating unresolved issues can be. If you express
   frustration — even mildly, e.g., "This is taking too long" or "I've been
   waiting for days" — our system automatically detects this and escalates
   your case to a Senior Support Agent or higher, depending on the situation.
   You don't need to repeat yourself; we're already on it.

Q10: I said "this is ridiculous" — will that affect my case?
A: Yes, in a positive way. Expressions of frustration or mild anger are treated
   as signals that you need more immediate human attention. Our escalation engine
   picks up on these phrases and routes your case to the appropriate team level
   so you get a faster, more personalised response.

Q11: What if my issue is urgent and needs to be fixed today?
A: Phrases like "I need this fixed today," "This is urgent," or "I need this
   sorted immediately" are detected as urgency signals. Your case will be
   escalated and prioritised accordingly. Please make sure to mention the
   specific issue so our team can prepare before reaching out.

Q12: I threatened to post about this on social media. What will happen?
A: Statements about filing complaints, contacting consumer forums, or posting
   on social media are taken seriously. Your case will be escalated to at least
   a Level 3 (Support Manager) and potentially Level 4 (Business Director),
   ensuring senior leadership is aware and takes personal responsibility for
   resolving your issue.

Q13: Is there a safety-related concern I can report through the chatbot?
A: Yes. If you have any safety-related concern — such as damaged equipment,
   electrical issues with a router, or anything that poses a risk to you or
   others — please describe it in the chat. Safety concerns are treated as the
   highest priority (Level 4) and are escalated to the Business Director
   immediately.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4: LEGAL & COMPLAINT-RELATED QUERIES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q14: I'm considering legal action. Will the chatbot handle this?
A: If you mention legal action, consulting a lawyer, or filing a formal
   complaint, your case is automatically escalated to a Support Manager or
   Business Director (Level 3 or 4). A senior representative will contact
   you directly to address your concern and work towards a resolution before
   things go further.

Q15: How do I file a formal complaint with Smart Route Service?
A: You can initiate a formal complaint through the chatbot by stating clearly
   that you wish to file a complaint. Your case will be escalated immediately
   to a Support Manager. You will receive a complaint reference number and a
   follow-up within the next business day.

Q16: What if my issue has not been resolved despite multiple contacts?
A: Repeated failed attempts to resolve an issue are flagged by our system.
   If your conversation history shows unresolved interactions, your case is
   automatically elevated to a Level 3 (Support Manager) escalation, ensuring
   fresh eyes and greater authority are applied to your problem.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5: CHATBOT LIMITATIONS & KNOWLEDGE GAPS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q17: What happens if the chatbot doesn't know the answer to my question?
A: If the chatbot cannot find a confident answer in our knowledge base, it will
   not guess or provide incorrect information. Instead, it will automatically
   escalate your query to a Tier 1 Support agent (Level 1) who has access to
   broader resources and can provide an accurate answer.

Q18: Why did the chatbot say it's connecting me to support even though I didn't
     ask?
A: Our system proactively escalates when it cannot confidently answer a query or
   detects emotional signals in the conversation. This ensures you always receive
   accurate, human-verified help rather than an incorrect automated response.

Q19: Can the chatbot handle billing or account-related issues?
A: The chatbot can answer general questions about billing, plans, and accounts.
   However, for sensitive actions — such as account changes, payment disputes,
   or billing discrepancies — the chatbot will connect you to a human agent who
   has the authority and tools to make those changes securely.

Q20: Is the chatbot available 24/7?
A: Yes, the Smart Route Service chatbot is available around the clock. You can
   start a conversation and have your issue escalated at any time of day or
   night. Human follow-ups will occur during business hours: Monday to Saturday,
   9:00 AM – 7:00 PM.

SECTION 6: DATA, PRIVACY & CONVERSATION RECORDS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q21: Is my chat conversation saved?
A: Yes. Conversation history is retained to provide context to our support
   agents when your case is escalated. This ensures you do not have to repeat
   yourself. Conversation data is used solely for support purposes and is
   handled in line with our privacy policy.

Q22: Who sees my conversation when my case is escalated?
A: When escalated, your conversation history is shared with the assigned support
   team member (Tier 1 agent, Senior Agent, Support Manager, or Business
   Director, depending on the level). This helps them understand the full
   context of your issue without you needing to re-explain everything.

Q23: Can I request that my conversation not be used?
A: For privacy-related requests, please speak directly with our support team.
   Contact us during business hours (Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM)
   and a representative will guide you through the process.

SECTION 7: TIPS FOR FASTER RESOLUTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q24: How can I get my issue resolved as quickly as possible?
A: To help us resolve your issue faster:
   - Clearly describe the problem (e.g., "My internet has been down since
     yesterday morning at 10 AM").
   - Provide your account number or registered phone number when asked.
   - Mention if the issue has happened before or if it is recurring.
   - Let us know if the issue is urgent and why (e.g., working from home,
     important meeting).
   The more context you provide, the better equipped our team will be to
   resolve your issue on the first contact.

Q25: What if I'm calm but my issue is still serious?
A: Escalation is not based solely on emotional tone. If you calmly describe a
   serious issue — such as a prolonged outage, financial impact, or a safety
   concern — our system assesses the content of your message and escalates
   to the appropriate level. You don't need to be upset for your issue to
   receive priority attention.

Q26: I've already spoken to someone before but the issue is still not fixed.
     What should I do?
A: Please mention in the chat that this is a recurring or previously reported
   issue and provide any reference numbers if available. Our system will
   escalate this as a repeated unresolved case (Level 3), which triggers a
   Support Manager review to ensure the issue is finally resolved.
